Dylan Wang (Actor) - On This Day

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 24, 2024

Full Name: Wang Hedi
Profession: Actor

Biography: Dylan Wang is a Chinese actor and singer known for his roles in Meteor Garden (2018) and Love Between Fairy and Devil (2022). He rose to fame after winning the reality television competition Super Idol in 2017.

Wang won Super Idol and immediately secured his first acting role as Daoming Si in Meteor Garden. This role positioned him as a promising young actor, and in 2018 he went on to participate in variety shows such as Phanta City and The Inn 2.

In the following years, Wang continued to work on various television projects. He starred in the second season of Ever Night (2020) and The Rational Life (2021). While he faced criticism for his performance in Miss The Dragon (2021), his career saw a further boost with the success of Love Between Fairy and Devil (2022).

Beyond acting, Wang has endorsed products and launched his own fashion brand, D.DESIRABLE, in 2022. He has a passion for basketball, participating in the 2024 NBA All-Star Celebrity Game.

Born: December 20, 1998
Birthplace: Chengdu, Sichuan, China
Age: 25 years old

Generation: Generation Z
Chinese Zodiac: Tiger
Star Sign: Sagittarius
