5 celebrities who have been to TB Joshua's Synagogue church

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

In most of these cases, celebrities take their problems to men of God to find solutions. Prophet T.B. Joshua has played host to many celebrities who are in search of a miracle.

1.Jim Iyke

Jim Iyke in 2013 visited the The Synagogue Church of all Nations. In a video which went viral, Jim Iyke was allegedly delivered from certain illness. Well in a recent interview, Jim Iyke gave a new twist to that incident, claiming he was misled by the man of God.

2.Hanks Anuku

Hanks Anuku is another celebrity who has also gotten some sort of healing from Prophet T.B. Joshua. Hanks Anuku revealed while granting an interview that he was healed of mental illness when he visited the man of God. He said he, had prior to visiting T.B. Joshua's church, visited hospitals to get healing but to no avail.

3.Munachi Abii

Munachi Abii is one pretty celebrity who doubles as the former Most Beautiful Girl In Nigeria. Well, she was once sighted at the Synagogue church. Till date, it has not been revealed what exactly she went to do at the church.

4.Ogenyi Onazi

ADVERTISEMENT

Ogenyi Onazi is one name every lover of football from Nigeria can easily identify. However, Ogenyi has a very close tie to Prophet T.B. Joshua and his church. It has been revealed that he started his football career at My People FC, which was founded and funded single-handedly by Prophet TB Joshua.

It was while playing at the Lagos club that Onazi was discovered and picked to represent Nigeria at the 2009 U-17 FIFA World Cup.

5.Joseph Yobo

Former Nigerian International Joseph Yobo reportedly visited the Synagogue Church of T.B. Joshua for prayers after suffering a hamstring injury in the 2010 Africa Cup of Nations. He went on to lift the 2013 African Cup Of nations, three years later before retiring from International football.
