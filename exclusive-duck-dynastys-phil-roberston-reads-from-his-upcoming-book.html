Exclusive: Duck Dynastys Phil Robertson Reads From His Upcoming Book

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 2, 2024

The star of A&E’s hit series — and the founder of the Louisiana-based Duck Commander retail empire — has written a book.  In Happy, Happy, Happy, Robertson writes about the wild and bumpy path he took before he found the success and peace of mind he enjoys today.

In an exclusive video, above, Phil talks about the book — and in the sound clip below, he reads an excerpt from the accompanying audiobook.

Happy, Happy, Happy goes on sale May 7.
