Meet Cody Fern: what is the American Horror Story star up to now?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Cody Fern is an actor, director, and screenplay writer popularly known for playing Michael Langdon in American Horror Story. He made his acting debut in a series of short movies such as Still Take You Home. His breakthrough came in 2017 when he played Jim Mason in The Tribes of Palos Verdes.

In 2014, Cody Fern won the Heath Ledger scholarship award. He used the prize money to move to Los Angeles to get acting gigs. He has been able to cement himself as one of the top young actors in Hollywood.

Where is Cody Fern from?

The actor is originally from Australia, and his nationality is Australian. He was born and raised in Southern Cross, Western Australia.

His hometown is small, with about 700 residents. Although he remains quiet about his parents and siblings, he says they are doing well back home.

What is Cody Fern famous for?

The actor, director, and screenplay writer is famous for his roles in American Crime Story, American Horror Story, and House of Cards. However, he did not gain instant Hollywood fame and success.

After completing his higher studies in Australia, he secured a well-paying job in the finance industry. He hated his life while working in finance.

At 22, he decided to quit the job to pursue a career in acting. He played the lead role of Albert in the National Theatre of Great Britain's production War Horse.

He joined a theatre group and was ready to make it big in the acting industry. He won the Heath Ledger Scholarship given to fast-rising Australian actors in 2014.

He used this scholarship's cash prize to move to Los Angeles, California, hoping to get acting gigs and improve his skills. He bagged small roles in a series of short movies.

What character did Cody Fern play in the American Horror Story?

Between 2018 and 2021, the actor played Valiant Thor, Xavier Plympton, and Michael Langdon in 21 episodes of American Horror Story.

In American Horror Story: Apocalypse, he played the role of Michael Langdon, an antichrist who was last seen as a toddler in Murder House, the first season of the series. In American Horror Story: 1984, he played Xavier Plympton.

Besides being an actor, Cody Fern is a director. He directed his first movie, Pieces, in which he played Charlie. He also wrote the short film.

He is also a model who has appeared on the cover of numerous magazines, including British GQ and Confidential magazine. In addition, he has worked for famous fashion designers and participated in fashion shows.

The actor has won three awards in his career.
