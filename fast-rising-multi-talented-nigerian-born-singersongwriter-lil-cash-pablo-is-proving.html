Fast rising Singer LIL CASH PABLO Otemuyiwa Oluwafemi was born Ogun state and popularly known by the stage name, Cash Pablo

The multi-talented singer, songwriter, was born in Nigeria, where he began his music career.

He was raised in Ogun State and has grown from that upcoming artist in Lagos Nigeria, many years ago.

Lil Cash Pablo has been topping charts since he released his official debut single BILLI MILLI accompanied by an exotic music video shot in Lagos state Nigeria Directed By WG FILMS

BILLI MILLI made its debut on iTunes and apple music chart and topped the chart 14 Days straight one month of release. A great record achieved for the first time by a contemporary Afrobeats artist.
