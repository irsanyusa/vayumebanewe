'I Can See Your Voice' Returns to FOX This Fall for Season 3

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing I Can See Your Voice

Cr: Michael Becker / FOX

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The hit music guessing game show I Can See Your Voice is returning for a third season on FOX! The show, which is hosted by Ken Jeong and features Cheryl Hines and Adrienne Bailon-Houghton as regular panelists, will premiere on September 19, 2023.

I Can See Your Voice is Renewed For a Third Season

In each episode of I Can See Your Voice, a contestant must try to determine which of the 12 mystery singers can actually sing. The show provides the contestant with clues about the singers’ singing ability, while also allowing them to ask the singers to perform certain tasks. At the end of the show, the contestant must choose one of the mystery singers to duet with a guest artist. If the mystery singer can sing, the contestant wins a prize.

Viewers loved the first two seasons of I Can See Your Voice, praising the show for its unique concept and its ability to entertain audiences of all ages. The show has also featured some amazing singers, including singer and songwriter Michael Bolton, and Dee Snider.

The third season of I Can See Your Voice is sure to be even more exciting than the first two. With new clues and challenges, the contestants will have their work cut out for them as they try to determine which of the mystery singers can actually sing.

So if you’re a fan of music, guessing games, or just plain old good TV, be sure to tune in to I Can See Your Voice when it returns. The season premiere will be on Fox on September 19, 2023, and then on Hulu the following day.

What Is The Cash Prize On I Can See Your Voice?

The cash prize on I Can See Your Voice varies by country. In the United States, the prize structure is as follows: contestants receive $10,000 for each bad singer eliminated and $100,000 for correctly guessing the good singer. This means the maximum prize in the U.S. is $200,000.

However, it’s important to note that contestants don’t automatically win $100,000 for guessing the good singer. They have a choice to make. They can either walk away with the money they have already won or bet it all on whether the last contestant is a good singer. If they bet correctly and the singer is good, they win $100,000. But if they bet and the singer is bad, they leave with nothing, and the singer gets the $100,000.

The cash prize on I Can See Your Voice serves as a significant incentive for contestants to guess the good singers accurately. However, it also poses a risk because contestants can lose all their winnings if they make the wrong guess.
