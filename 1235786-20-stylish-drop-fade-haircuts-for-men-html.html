What is a drop fade?

A drop fade cut bears a lot of similarities to a regular fade haircut. However, the difference is that it becomes lower around the front hairline and at the back of a person's head. The reason why it has some benefits compared to the sisterly style is the sharpness when it comes to outlining the face and some more depth in the crown area. Drop fade is a universal hairstyle which works together with plenty of bases, from the pompadour to afro. It can make everything more fresh and creative, that is why men from all around the world fancy it so much.
