Best Jeans for Petite Women: Tested and Reviewed by Women Under 54

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 27, 2024

If you’re a petite woman like me, you know that every millimeter counts. Whether it’s a heel, the length of a skirt, or the tailoring to get the perfect hem on a pair of jeans, the right fit is essential to those of us under 5’4”. But being petite is just one factor in finding the fit of your perfect jeans. We found the best pairs of jeans designed specifically for petite women so you can find the fit that’s right for you.

How to choose the best jeans for petite women

The inseam

Most people under 5’ 4” will want to opt for an inseam between 25 - 27 inches. Go even shorter if you’re under five feet. The inseam also varies by leg length, so pay attention to how the jeans fit you at the hip, knee, and ankle.

The rise

This is a personal choice based on comfort and aesthetics. Low-rise jeans hit well below the navel, around 7 to 8 inches from the crotch. Mid-rise qualifies as 8 to 10 inches and high-rise is 10 inches or above.

The cut

Boyfriend or Mom jeans have a boxier, more relaxed fit while skinny and straight-leg jeans are more fitted. Flare and bootcut jeans fit right between with a curvier hip and flared lower leg.

The wash

Blue jeans can come in many shades of indigo, such as light denim, medium denim, or dark denim. They come in other washes as well, like black, gray, white, ecru, or colors like pink and purple.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

What does “petite” mean in jeans?

Petite sizes are designed for women 5 feet 4 inches and under, but it’s not just about cutting the hem shorter. Petite jeans raise the waist, hip, knee, and hem to fit a shorter profile. Petite sizes aim to fit smaller frames in a more balanced way.

What type of jeans look best on petite women?

Finding petite pants that fit comfortably and accentuate your figure in the right way can be challenging. Being petite is just one part of your fit profile, so here are a few things to consider. Skinny cuts that elongate the figure are popular among petite women as they can create a more extended look with a mid-rise or high-rise waist. When wearing skinny jeans, ensure the knee area aligns with your natural bend. A baggy knee makes jeans look ill-fitting.

A petite boot cut jean emphasizes the hips and creates an hourglass figure for those with an athletic figure. The curviness in the boot cut adds contour to the frame.

More baggy style, or “Mom jeans,” work well for medium- and large-build petites. If you’re very slight, the bagginess might overwhelm your frame, so opt for more streamlined cuts. On the other hand, curvier or broader types might look quite streamlined in Mom-style jeans. The high waist is also known to create a flatter midsection.

What is the best jeans length for petite women?

Best length varies by person. Some people like denim hemmed right at the ankle, while others look best with a cropped look. Lengthening the leg will make you look taller, so consider a hemline beneath the ankle bone with a pointy-toe flat, wedge shoe, or high heel.

What is the difference between short and petite jeans?

Short jeans simply shorten the hemline, while petite jeans balance the decrease in size at the hem, waistline, and sometimes thigh area. If you prefer a regular cut with a shorter hem, you can alter your jeans or buy a “short” length.

Can petites wear high-waisted jeans?

Yes, and as a matter of fact, they are often very flattering. Raising the waist gives the illusion of a longer leg. However, you might look more balanced with mid-rise or low-rise jeans if you have a shorter torso and longer legs.
