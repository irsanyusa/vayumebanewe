Photos From Junior Popes Burial Surface, Trigger Emotions: Life Is Just Useless

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 17, 2024
  • Heartbreaking images from the ongoing burial of the late actor, John Paul Odonwodo, aka Junior Pope, have surfaced online
  • His widow was dressed in all black with dark shades on while holding her three boys close
  • Another picture saw people who seemed like his family members sitting close to the deceased's coffin while in church

The burial ceremony of Nigerian actor John Paul Odonwodo, aka Junior Pope, who died a month ago, is ongoing. The first set of photos from the event has emerged on social media, sparking reactions.

The actor's burial ceremony is taking place at his hometown, Ukehe, in Nsukka, Igboetiti LGA of Enugu state.

Junior Pope's first son looks emotional

The sons of the deceased are yet to fully come to terms with the death of their beloved father.
